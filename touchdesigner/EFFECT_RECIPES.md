# Effect Recipes

These recipes are built around the richer OSC channels in `v1.1.0`.

## Aurora Bloom

- Base color from `main/hue` and `main/accent_hue`
- Halo size from `main/halo`
- Trail brightness from `main/ribbon`
- Feedback opacity from `main/pulse`

Look:

- soft cyan and mint gradients
- wide halo with elegant ribbons
- calmer motion with a dreamy bloom

## Solar Flare

- Core size from `main/pinch`
- Bloom gain from `main/flare`
- Shock flashes from `main/burst`
- Glow warmth from `main/color_r`, `main/color_g`

Look:

- gold, orange, and white-hot flashes
- pinch-driven core compression
- dramatic release hits

## Neon Prism

- Hue split between `main/hue` and `main/accent_hue`
- UV rotation from `main/spin`
- Sparkle amount from `main/shimmer`
- Turbulence from `main/turbulence`

Look:

- shifting pink-blue-cyan banding
- flashy specular particles
- very “music visualizer” energy

## Toxic Plasma

- Green/yellow palette rows from `palette_id` `4`
- Noise amplitude from `main/turbulence`
- Pulse from `main/pulse`
- Ribbon weight from `main/ribbon`

Look:

- radioactive vapor and electric arcs
- brighter on faster hand motion
- aggressive, liquid-feeling trails

## Ice Cathedral

- Cool palette rows from `palette_id` `0`
- Halo and spread from `main/halo` and `main/radius`
- Shimmer from `main/shimmer`
- Depth layering from `main/depth`

Look:

- frosted blue-white structure
- airy, spacious rings
- cleaner and more elegant than the louder looks

## Binary Portal

- Two-hand midpoint from `fusion/x` and `fusion/y`
- Bridge beam from `fusion/bridge`
- Midpoint bloom from `fusion/bloom`
- Shape tension from `fusion/vortex` and `fusion/chaos`

Look:

- portal beam between hands
- intense midpoint orb when hands converge
- ideal for two-hand “summoning” moments
